About

Emanuelle was born in Italy, precisely in Alatri, a small town 60km south of Rome. From an early age he had a passion for technology and for design in general. He began his small career in the world of web design way back in 2012 when he discovered Photoshop. A career that was put aside when he moved to Romania in 2015. After high school he started the finance and banking university which he later dropped out because it did not correspond to his way of seeing things. Currently he has returned to his great passion, web design but this time with a plus, the front-end developing. For the future he would like to get hold of and learn back-end developing.
